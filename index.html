Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Smart Typing.

Is Smart Typing free to use?

Yes. Core typing practice is completely free. Premium features will be available in future updates.

Which languages are supported?

English, Hindi and Marathi. More languages will be added in future versions.

Can I practice for government typing exams?

Yes. Dedicated government exam passages and timed practice modes will be available.

Do I need to create an account?

No. You can practice without logging in. Login will be required only for history, leaderboard, certificates and cloud sync.

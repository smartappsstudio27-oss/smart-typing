Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

SmartApps Studio is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have while using Smart Typing.

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

Data Collection

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Depending on the features you use, we may collect information that you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, feedback, contact messages and account details.

Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect technical information including browser type, operating system, device information, screen size, language preference, pages visited, session duration and diagnostic logs.

Typing Statistics

Smart Typing stores typing performance such as WPM, Net WPM, Accuracy, Error Count, Test History and Practice Statistics to improve your experience.

Data Usage

How We Use Your Information

  • Improve website performance and usability.
  • Save your typing progress and statistics.
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries.
  • Detect technical problems and improve reliability.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud and unauthorized access.
  • Develop new features and enhance user experience.
Cookies

Cookies & Local Storage

Smart Typing may use browser cookies, local storage and similar technologies to remember your preferences, selected theme, practice settings and recently completed typing sessions.

These technologies help improve performance and provide a smoother user experience without repeatedly asking for the same preferences.

Log Data

Like many websites, our servers or third-party services may collect standard log information such as IP address, browser version, operating system, referral pages, access time and diagnostic information.

Analytics & Performance Monitoring

In future versions, Smart Typing may use Google Analytics or similar services to understand how users interact with the website, identify popular features and improve overall performance.

Analytics data is used only for improving the platform and understanding usage trends.

Advertising

Google AdSense

Smart Typing may display advertisements provided by Google AdSense and other trusted advertising partners.

These advertising providers may use cookies or similar technologies to display relevant advertisements, measure campaign performance and improve the advertising experience.

You can manage advertising preferences through your Google account settings where applicable.

Authentication

Firebase Services

Future versions of Smart Typing may use Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore for secure user accounts, cloud synchronization, leaderboards and typing history.

Information stored through Firebase will be protected using Google's security infrastructure and authentication mechanisms.

Third-Party Services

External Service Providers

  • Google Analytics (Future)
  • Google AdSense
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Cloudflare CDN
  • GitHub Pages Hosting

These services operate according to their own privacy policies and terms of service.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction.

Although we strive to use commercially acceptable methods, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Smart Typing is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children below the age required under applicable laws without appropriate parental or guardian consent.

Your Rights

  • Request access to your data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account where applicable.
  • Manage cookies through your browser settings.
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns.

Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and improve the Smart Typing platform.

Policy Updates

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, applicable laws or technology.

The latest version will always be published on this page with the updated revision date.

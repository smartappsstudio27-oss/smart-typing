About Smart Typing

Building the Future of Professional Typing Practice

Smart Typing is a modern online typing practice platform developed by SmartApps Studio. Our mission is to provide a fast, accurate, responsive and feature-rich typing experience for students, professionals and competitive exam aspirants.

Our Mission

Making Professional Typing Practice Accessible to Everyone

Our mission is to build a modern, fast and reliable typing platform that helps users improve their typing speed, accuracy and overall productivity through structured practice.

We believe typing is an essential digital skill for students, developers, accountants, government exam aspirants, office professionals and anyone who uses a computer regularly.

Smart Typing focuses on practical learning, detailed performance analysis and continuous improvement instead of simple speed testing.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Smart Typing free to use?

Yes. The core typing practice features are available free of charge. Additional premium features may be introduced in future versions.

Which languages are supported?

Smart Typing is designed to support English, Hindi and Marathi. More languages will be added in future updates.

Will my progress be saved?

Yes. Practice history is stored locally. Future versions will synchronize progress securely using Firebase.

Can I use Smart Typing on mobile?

Absolutely. The website is fully responsive and optimized for desktop, tablet and mobile devices.
